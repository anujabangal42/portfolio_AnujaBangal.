About Me

I am a passionate Software Engineer, Full Stack Developer, and AI/ML enthusiast who enjoys building real-world applications and continuously learning new technologies.

Currently, I am pursuing a Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Engineering from Dr. D. Y. Patil Technical Campus, Pune (2024 – 2027), with a current SGPA of 8.77.

I have completed my Diploma in Information Technology from MIT World Peace University, Pune (2021 – 2024), where I secured 83% overall.

I completed my Secondary School Certificate (SSC) from Shri Sainath High School, Alkuti, Ahilyanagar in 2021, achieving 90.60%.

I have a strong foundation in Java, Python, Data Structures, Object-Oriented Programming, DBMS, and Operating Systems. I also have hands-on experience in web development using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Angular, along with backend integration using JDBC and MySQL.

I have worked on impactful projects such as PharmaGuard, a machine learning-based pharmacogenomic risk prediction system, and EduTech, a smart career recommendation platform developed using Python and Streamlit. I am also currently developing a mobile-based EHR system for ASHA workers to improve healthcare accessibility in low-internet areas.

Additionally, I have completed a web development internship at Prodigy InfoTech, where I built responsive web pages and enhanced user interface performance. I am also a Smart India Hackathon 2025 Finalist, demonstrating my ability to solve real-world problems through innovative solutions.

I am a quick learner, a team player, and highly motivated to apply my technical skills in a dynamic environment while continuously growing as a software engineer.
